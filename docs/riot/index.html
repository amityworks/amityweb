#riot

Why use Riot?

Riot is an open source collaboration app built on the Matrix.org open standard for interoperable communication: supporting group chat, file transfer, voice and video calling, integrations to other apps, bridges to other communication systems and much more.

Please note that Riot requires Chrome, Firefox or Safari on the web, or iOS or Android on mobile.
